In-house & third party integrations

Our advanced APIs allow us to seamlessly connect with your existing IT infrastructure and third-party tools that you work with every day.

We provide seamless integrations with your organization’s authentication system through single sign-on (Active Directories, OpenID, SAML, CAS, Shibboleth,etc.) to allow users to sign in using your organization’s login credentials.

We also have a wide range of pre-built integrations with popular applications including various payment gateways, calendar systems, CRMs, analytics tools, video-conferencing apps, Reserve with Google (Google’s discovery and book platform), and more.

Dedicated support & account management

No matter what your support requirements are, we have the capability to offer all levels of support to your various user groups round the clock through email, phone or chat. We also provide support SLAs to ensure quick response and resolution times.

A dedicated account manager will also be assigned to your project and will be the single point of contact to assist you with any queries or troubleshooting.

Private and on-premise hosting

We can offer on-premise hosting within your organization’s data centers or cloud hosting on private servers in a region and hosting-provider of your preference.

With the application hosted on dedicated servers, you’ll have the added flexibility, security and scalability to handle higher load and more API calls. We can also offer uptime SLAs as per your requirement.

A collaborative and transparent implementation approach that keeps your goals at the forefront

From the kick-off meeting with our enterprise specialists to the final go-live, our multi-step consultative process ensures that we deliver exactly what you need!

01: Requirement Understanding & Consultation Phase

After the contract signing, our team will meet with your key stakeholders to take a deeper dive into your specific scheduling use cases, custom workflows, integrations with your existing systems, and other project requirements

02: Design Phase

Our team works with your key stakeholders on designing the system & hosting architecture, backend and frontend of the enterprise scheduler. Regular feedback is taken and the system design and timeline is finalized

03: Development Phase

Based on the finalized system design and project requirements, our development team builds your custom enterprise appointment solution. This will also involve integrating with your internal and third-party system.

04: Testing & Feedback Phase

After development, the application is released for user acceptance testing. The feedback given by your team is discussed and incorporated

05: Training Phase

For maximum product adoption, different user groups are trained through on-site sessions, webinars, videos, user manuals, FAQs, help guides, etc. We also offer various training models like “train the trainer”, facilitator-led direct user training, etc.

06: Deployment Phase

Once the application is accepted and users are trained, it is deployed in a live environment. We support both phased roll-out and big-bang implementation (all users go-live at once). Our team will hand-hold you every step of the way to ensure a successful deployment

07: Maintenance & Support Phase

Post deployment, our team will continuously monitor application performance, and regularly push security and compatibility patches, feature updates or any other product improvements

Key features in our enterprise appointment scheduling software solution

Omnichannel Scheduling

Accept bookings from Google/ social media, or integrate our 24x7 online booking portal on your client-facing application/ website to provide a seamless booking experience

User Roles & Rights

With role-based access you can control which feature or how much information is available to different users depending on their role within the organization

Organizational Hierarchy

Easily manage cross functional teams, multiple locations and users spread across different geographies from a single interface

Database Management

Store important client data like appointment details, purchase history, etc. under dedicated user profiles to collect insights and deliver personalized services

Reporting & Analytics

Keep a track of your bookings, users, and other important metrics with the option to print and export data in various formats

Reminders & Notifications

Receive sms, email, or in-app notifications for booking updates. Automated reminders are also sent to attendees before appointments to reduce no shows

Mobile App

Stay on top of your upcoming appointments and schedule no matter where you are with Appointy’s Android & iOS mobile app

Online payments
