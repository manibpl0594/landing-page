Reserve with Google helps New York-based barbershop earn $1,700 additional revenue every month

Name: Barber’s Blueprint

Location: New York

Industry: Barber

Arthur’s story

Arthur Iskhakov started Barber’s Blueprint in 2011 with a mission to open an affordable yet trendy barbershop from where customers walk out feeling good about themselves.

A few years after he opened up, Arthur started using Appointy to reduce the number of phone calls which interfered with his business, but needed a way to get more customers.

THE PROBLEM

Acquiring new customers for barbershops on a fixed budget

It’s often tough for barbers to find the right marketing channel that helps them increase clientele and is also affordable. In a competitive industry like hair & beauty, where potential customers have a number of options to choose from, how do you stand out and get more customers than your competitors?

So when Arthur was looking for ways to get new customers for his business, he saw the option to get listed on Reserve with Google in his Appointy account for free and opted-in for the service.

In just a couple of days of getting listed on Reserve with Google, Arthur started getting additional bookings from Google.

Reserve with Google allows you to discover, book, and pay for classes and appointments: all of it, right from Google. It’s pretty easy to opt-in, hassle-free, and doesn’t take much effort

THE SOLUTION

Use Reserve with Google to get discovered and booked by new customers for free

Barbers Blueprint was among the first few beauty businesses in the US to get listed on Reserve with Google. An early listing on the platform gave them an edge over their competitors and helped them get discovered among new potential customers without much competition.

Barber’s Blueprint’s Google Reserve listing has their business photos, website link, and Google reviews as well. When a potential customer interacts with all these resources, especially reads their positive online reviews, that’s when they are in a hyper-engaged state.

When people search on Google and they see our business in the search results, rather than calling us using the ‘Call Now’ option, they can press ‘Book Online’ and directly book an appointment with us.

THE RESULT

Over $36k additional revenue from Reserve with Google bookings

Due to their popularity on Reserve with Google, Barber’s Blueprint was featured as one of the RwG-recommended popular locations for haircut in New York. This pins their booking link to the RwG home page giving them preferred visibility over other competitors.
