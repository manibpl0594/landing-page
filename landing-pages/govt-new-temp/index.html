Secure and easy-to-use scheduling software for local, state, & federal government offices

Appointy can help cater to multiple use-cases and complex workflows for public agencies, offices, and their sub-departments

  • City Councils

    County offices and city councils can give citizens flexibility to schedule appointments, council meetings and events.

  • Embassies & Consulates

    Visa and passport offices can schedule new/ renewal passport requests, lost/ damaged passports, travelling visa services etc.

  • Public Sector Banks

    Banks can schedule appointments for new accounts, loan applications, investment assistance, bank account services etc.

  • Libraries

    Public, school or university libraries can manage bookings of study rooms, computer systems, hourly reading slots, and more

  • Department of Motor Vehicles

    DMVs can let citizens reserve time-slots to apply for new driving licenses, give driving tests, get a vehicle registration certificate, etc.

  • Marriage License Office

    Marriage license offices can allow citizens to book an appointment for ceremonial services, apply for a marriage license, and more.

  • Municipal Courts

    Courts can schedule in-person and virtual hearings for criminal cases, eviction cases, probation services, criminal records and more.

  • Public Facilities

    Parks and recreation centers can schedule events and allow public to book facilities like buildings, rooms, fields, courts etc.

Provide a seamless booking experience using your branded self-service booking portal

Let citizens pre-book appointment slots online 24x7. Make your booking portal available in 32+ languages to ensure inclusivity and connect with citizens in their local language.

Share your booking link via emails, SMS, brochures, etc. Allow website visitors to book your services directly by adding a booking widget to your site without any tech support.

Use our intelligent routing algorithms to match your citizen appointment requests with the most suitable officer based on their availability, schedules or qualification.

Save hours spent in long queues and reduce appointment no-shows

Send automated reminders and booking notifications to ensure that citizens reach your office at the right time and save hours spent in long queues. This also gives them the flexibility to plan their day and reschedule, if needed.

Customize the notification content to add appointment instructions, location links, department info, required documents & proofs, etc.

Offer virtual appointments and host online events, and webinars

You can also conduct virtual events to share your ideas, plans, and vision for the constituency with a wider audience and expand reach.

Citizens can easily sign up for the virtual event online and the videoconference details will be sent to them via email or text.
